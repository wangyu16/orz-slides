Architecture

The pipeline runs in three stages — ingest, transform, serve — each independently scalable behind a queue.

  • Ingest buffers bursts and applies back-pressure.
  • Transform is stateless and horizontally scaled.
  • Serve reads from a cached materialized view.

Result: p99 latency under 40 ms at peak.

Key takeaway: narrow PDI across all methods.
